MovieLibs

MovieLibs is a place where you can create your movie libraries. In other words, you create your own specialized libraries which consists of your favourite movies. Actually, there is a little more to it that not only you can create your movie libraries but you can also share it with your friends, family or even foes that is so that they can know what fun they are missing by being enemies with you. It is a fun website, where you create your movie libraries as many as you want based on your genre and what not. And not only that you can control whether people can see your library your not, a special thing for the introvert users. Simply put, you can make your library either public or private and have privacy.

How to Use MovieLibs

MovieLibs has a pretty simple UI to begin with, and if you follow some simple steps, it will be a fun journey for all of us.

  1. Signup with your username, email and a pasword and if you already have one, just login.
  2. On the home page you are provided with some movie suggestions to add yo your library, other than that you can also use our search bar to search for your favourite movies and add them to your library.
  3. Then to add a movie to your a movie library you need to have a preexisting library, if yes then just click on add to your library button, which is at the bottom of the movie card.
  4. But if you don't have any preexisting library then just create one by clicking on create a new library option present on the navigation bar or when you click on the add to your library button you will also get an option for creating a new library.
  5. To create a movie library you need to put a name as well as it's visibility whether it should be public or private to you.
  6. To share your library just click on share your library tab from the navigation bar, there you will see all the libraries you have created and the movies you have added in them, there you have the option to share your library via a link just click on copy link and share. And even more you can change the visibilty of the library from there only.
  7. At last, once you have spent a lovely time on our site, do forget to login again.
